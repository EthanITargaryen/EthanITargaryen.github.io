About me

Peace be upon you. All praises belong to God, the Majestic, the Sublime.

I am Sheikh Azizul Hakim, currently persuing a CS undergrad degree in BUET.

I am passionate about a research oriented career in theoretical computer science, with a focus in bioinformatics. What I know about them is a droplet, what people know is a gallon perhaps, and what we do not know is greater than the Pacific.

Apart from my CS academics, I love to read about philosophy, jurisprudence, theology, mystics. I deeply adore the mystic/spiritual genre of poetry (like Song Offerings, or works of Rumi).

I am a full-time optimist, trying to find light even in the darkest of times. I believe that an eternal sense of goodness rests in each of us, and we need to cherish that.

I also like to travel, but not quite adventurously. I have a bit of interest in medieval architecture in Bangladesh. Also, I like cooking, and, I love hibiscuses.
